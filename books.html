Favorite Books

Here are some of my most favorite books of all time

A Discovery of Witches by Deborah Harkenss

This book is about Diana Bishop, a Yale professor conducting research at the University of Oxford. She is also a witch, though she prefers to forget about that part of her life and limits her interaction with other witches. She discovers a lost manuscript at the Bodleian Library which attracts the attention of others who have been searching for the manuscript. Here, she meets Matthew Clairmont, a vampire. This book is about Diana discovering herself, friends and enemies in the magical community of witches, vampires and demons.

Goodreads page of "A Discovery of Witches"

Magic Bites by Ilona Andrews

This has to be my favorite series ever. It managed to dislodge Harry Potter from the first place where it had been collecting dust for a decade. Magic Bites is completely unique book. It takes place in a world where magic and technology exist together. When magic worked, technology wouldn't and when technology worked, magic wouldn't. Kate Daniels is also the perfect heroine ever. She's sarcastic and hilarious and completely awesome. There are supernatural creatures you've never even heard and not just your normal werewolves and vampires. I'd also like to warn you not to read this book in a public setting, because you'll end up laughing so hard, people will think you're crazy.

Goodreads page of "Magic Bites"

Darkfever by Karen Marie Moning

If it's not absolutely clear yet that I love fantasy novels, here's another one. Darkfever uses Irish folklore, something I've never read in a book. It's the story of the fae people, commonly known as fairies. Now, don't get me wrong, these are the "Bibbidy Bobbidy Boo" turn the pumpkin into a carraige kinds. These are killer fae with a massive grudge against humans. Mac is another strong-willed power heroine in an action packed, fast paced series. I read the first 9 books in 2 weeks, just to give you an idea of the addiction.
